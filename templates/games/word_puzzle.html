Word Puzzle Instructions

Unscramble words in French and English to score points!

  • Game starts with French words by default
  • After 3 wrong attempts on a word, it will show you the English equivalent
  • The game will automatically switch to English words if you're struggling
  • Type your answer in the text box and submit
  • Use the hint button if you're stuck (but it will reduce your score)
  • All hints include English translations in parentheses
  • Solve as many words as possible before time runs out

Tips & Tricks
  • French words are simple everyday terms (maison, chat, jour, etc.)
  • In French, look for patterns like 'eau', 'eux', 'tion', or 'ment'
  • After 3 failed attempts, the game will help you with the English word
  • English words are shown in the hints in parentheses e.g. "cat", "house"
  • First identify vowels, then try to build words around them
  • The quicker you solve, the higher your score!
  • Don't worry if French is challenging - the game will adapt to your skill level
